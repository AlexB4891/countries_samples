1.5 Diff-in-diff design (unsaturated model)

\[Y_{it} = \beta_0 \times Treatment_{i} + \beta_1 \times Post_{t} + \beta_2 \times Treatment_{i} \times Post_{t} + \epsilon_{it}\]
