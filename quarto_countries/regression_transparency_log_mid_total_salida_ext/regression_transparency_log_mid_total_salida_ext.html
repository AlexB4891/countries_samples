Where:

  • \(\{ k = 2012, \ldots, 2017 \}\)

  • \(Time_{i}\) are dummy variables that are 1 if the year is \(k\), and 0 otherwise. The year 2014 is the reference year and therefore does not appear in the equation.
  • \(\epsilon_{it}\) is the error term.
